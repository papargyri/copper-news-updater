# ⏱️ Trade Feasibility & Timing Analysis

This report investigates whether there is enough time to actually buy copper *after* the news breaks, or if the market has already reacted.

## Price Action Around a News Spike (>6 articles)

We analyzed 108 days with high news volume to see exactly *when* the price moves.

- **Day 0 (The day the articles are published):** 0.31% return during the day
- **Day 1 (The day after publication):** -0.01% return
- **Day 2:** 0.07% return

**Conclusion:** The market reacts *immediately*. On days when lots of news is published, the price jumps significantly *that same day* (Day 0). If you read the news at the end of the day or the next morning, you have already missed the core upward movement, and you are buying into the subsequent flatline/decay (Day 1 & Day 2).
