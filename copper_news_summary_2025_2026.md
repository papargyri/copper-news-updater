# 🏗️ Copper News & Market Analysis Summary (Sept 2025 – Feb 2026)

Based on research conducted between September 2025 and February 2026, this report summarizes the critical drivers of the copper market and identifies key news reports from major global outlets.

## 📊 Market Overview
The copper market experienced a **"Triple Threat"** of demand colliding with significant supply shocks, leading to record-breaking prices in early 2026.

### 📉 Key Themes
| Theme | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **🚀 Surging Demand** | Driven by the "electrification of everything." A new massive driver is **AI Data Centers**, which can require 10x the copper of traditional facilities. |
| **⚠️ Supply Shocks** | Major disruptions at the **Grasberg mine** (mudslide), **Escondida** (strikes), and **Mantoverde** have shifted the market into a structural deficit. |
| **💰 Record Prices** | Prices shattered records in January 2026, peaking at nearly **$13,000 - $14,500 per ton** on the LME. |
| **🌍 Geopolitics** | Focus on **"Trumponomics"** (US tariffs on semi-finished copper) and the strategic race to secure critical mineral supply chains. |
